📋 Staff Rota Manager - Help & Documentation

🔄 Generate Monthly Rota

Create and manage monthly staff rotas with ease.

How to Access:

Staff Rota → 🔄 Generate Monthly Rota

Features:

  1. 📅 Create Monthly Sheets: Generate new monthly rota sheets
    • Select specific months or use quarter shortcuts (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
    • Choose year from dropdown
    • Automatically imports all staff from Settings and existing sheets
    • Sets up annual leave entitlements and formulas
  2. 🎯 Generate Monthly Rota: Apply shift patterns to existing sheets
    • Select from various rota patterns (7-day cycle, 14-day cycle, etc.)
    • Customize staffing levels for different shift types
    • Preview before applying to sheets
💡 Tip: Use "Create Monthly Sheets" first to set up your base sheets, then use "Generate Monthly Rota" to apply shift patterns.

🏥 Log New Absence

Record staff absences and automatically update monthly rotas.

How to Access:

Staff Rota → 🏥 Log New Absence

What it Does:

  1. Creates record in Absence Tracker sheet
  2. Automatically updates monthly rota sheets with absence codes:
    • AL - Annual Leave (paid)
    • SICK - Sick Leave (unpaid but counted)
    • TRAINING - Training (paid and counted)
  3. Handles multi-day absences across multiple months
  4. Updates payroll calculations automatically

📊 View Absence Reports

Generate reports and statistics on staff absences.

How to Access:

Staff Rota → 📊 View Absence Reports

Available Reports:

  • Staff absence summaries
  • Monthly absence statistics
  • Annual leave tracking
  • Sick leave analysis

⚙️ Settings

Configure staff members, roles, and system preferences.

How to Access:

Staff Rota → ⚙️ Settings

Configuration Options:

  • Add/edit staff members
  • Set annual leave entitlements
  • Configure shift types and codes
  • Manage email notifications

🚀 Getting Started

  1. First Time Setup:
    • Go to ⚙️ Settings to add your staff members
    • Set annual leave entitlements for each staff member
  2. Create Your First Rota:
    • Open 🔄 Generate Monthly Rota
    • Use 📅 Create Monthly Sheets to generate sheets for your desired months
    • Use 🎯 Generate Monthly Rota to apply shift patterns
  3. Manage Absences:
    • Use 🏥 Log New Absence to record any staff absences
    • Check 📊 View Absence Reports for summaries
💡 Pro Tip: The system automatically calculates hours worked, sick days, training days, and remaining annual leave for payroll purposes.
