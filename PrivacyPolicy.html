Quick Summary: Staff Rota Manager operates entirely within your Google Sheets. Your data never leaves Google's servers, and we only access the minimum permissions needed to function. No personal data is collected or stored by us.

1. Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Rota Data: Staff names, shift assignments, and absence information you enter
  • Settings: Your preferences for the add-on functionality
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about feature usage (optional)

Data We Don't Collect

  • Personal contact information beyond what you enter in your rota
  • Financial information
  • Data from other Google services
  • Your Google account credentials

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the Staff Rota Manager functionality:

  • Creating and managing rota sheets within your Google Sheets
  • Calculating work hours and absence statistics from your entered data
  • Applying shift patterns and bulk operations as requested
  • Providing absence reports and analytics based on your data
Important: All processing happens within Google's infrastructure. We never transfer your data to external servers.

3. Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored

  • Google Sheets: All rota data remains in your Google Sheets
  • Google's Servers: Temporary processing data is handled by Google Apps Script
  • Your Device: Settings and preferences may be cached locally

Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Access is restricted to the specific Google Sheet you're working with
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • Google's security infrastructure protects your data

4. Permissions and Access

Staff Rota Manager requests the following permissions:

  • View and manage spreadsheets: To create rota sheets and manage shift data
  • Display and run third-party web content: To show the user interface
  • Connect to external services: For future integrations (not currently used)
  • Know your basic profile info: For user identification and support
    • Scope Limitation: We only access the Google Sheets where you use this add-on. We cannot access other spreadsheets or Google services.

    5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

    We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data.
